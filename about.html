About TGFC

Three generations of family expertise in the food industry, built on relationships and driven by results.

Our History

Built on Legacy

Gary and Mitch Goldberger grew up in their families' vertically integrated meat business. The cousins both joined Goldberger Foods in 1984.

At the time, GFI was fabricating cattle, grinding ground beef, further processing ground and breaded items, cutting steaks and cooking roasts.

In 2016, Gary and Mitch joined together and started Third Generation Food Company, LLC — bringing decades of industry knowledge to a new venture.

Our Journey

Key Milestones

1984

Joined the Family Business

Gary and Mitch join Goldberger Foods, learning the vertically integrated meat business from the ground up.

1989

Federal Beef Processors

Entered the packinghouse business, opening two facilities. FBP harvested and fabricated over 1,500 head of cattle per day.

2016

Third Generation Food Company

Gary and Mitch founded TGFC, combining 60+ years of combined experience into a vertically integrated, bricks-and-mortarless food company.

Today

Growing Every Day

Multiple proprietary product lines, strategic co-packing partnerships, and a growing customer base across all market segments.

What We Do
